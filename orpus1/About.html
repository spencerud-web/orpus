About Orpus

Orpus is building a smarter way to track lab supplies and resources, helping researchers spend less time organizing materials and more time making progress.

Our platform is designed to make inventory management simpler, faster, and more reliable for busy scientific teams.

How It Works

Tracking that runs itself. Orpus infers reagent usage from the protocols your team is already running — so nobody has to learn a new habit.

1

Define your protocols

Write or import your experiments and tag each step with reagents and volumes — just once. Orpus version-controls every change automatically.

2

Log a protocol run

Mark a protocol as run and Orpus records the reagents it used automatically — every quantity decremented, with no separate logging step.

3

Get explainable alerts

Smart alerts tell you what to reorder and when, with a clear paper trail: run 12× at 200µL, you have about 40% of your Trypsin left.
