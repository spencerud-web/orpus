About Orpus

Orpus is building a smarter way to track lab supplies and resources, helping researchers spend less time organizing materials and more time making progress.

Our platform is designed to make inventory management simpler, faster, and more reliable for busy scientific teams.

What We Focus On

Clarity Keep track of what your lab has, where it is, and when it needs attention.
Efficiency Reduce wasted time and eliminate avoidable supply shortages.
Reliability Create a dependable system for teams that need consistency and accuracy.
